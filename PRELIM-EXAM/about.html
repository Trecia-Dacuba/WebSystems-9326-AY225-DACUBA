About Philippine Red Cross

A legacy of compassion and service spanning over seven decades

Our History

The Philippine Red Cross was established on March 22, 1947, by virtue of Republic Act No. 95. As an independent, non-profit organization, we serve as an auxiliary to the government in humanitarian services and civil defense preparedness.

For over 75 years, we have been at the forefront of disaster response, health services, and community development, touching millions of lives across the archipelago.
